About the Project Publication reference: EuropeAid/134029/IH/SER/TR

Start date: 24 August 2015

Implementation period: 24 months

End date: 24 August 2017

Beneficiary: Capital Markets Board of Turkey

Contracting authority: Central Finance and Contracts Unit (CFCU), Turkey

Contractor: A consortium led by Ernst & Young et Associés (EY France) and composed of EY France, EY Turkey, Expertise France and Havas Wordwide

Financing: General budget of the EU (90 %) and Turkish national contribution (10 %).

The overall objective of the project is to improve the availability of investment via capital markets. The purpose of this project is to improve the institutional and regulatory capacities of the CMB to supervise capital markets and institutions in line with EU. The results to be achieved are as follow:

Result 1: The capacity of specialisation and expertise of CMB experts on EU accounting and auditing legislation as well as EU countries’ IFRS application is improved.

Result 2: Alignment with the EU Acquis will be increased and administrative and institutional capacity of CMB is improved.

Result 3: Public awareness is increased.
