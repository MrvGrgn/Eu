Events

Public Awareness

New European Union Project has been started by the Capital Market Board.

Promotion of the European Union Project for “Technical Assistance for Strengthening the Capital Markets Board” has been organised by the launch event held on 7 December 2015 in Ankara. Opening speeches have been given by Mr. Stephane Kherroubi, the Project Director of Ernst & Young France as one of the project partners, Undersecretary Balazs Gergya, the Director of Commerce, Economy and Agriculture Department of EU Turkey Delegation, Ms. Emine Döğer, the Program Authorization Officer and Deputy Director of the Central Finance and Contracts Unit, and the Chairman of the Capital Market Board, Dr. Vahdettin Ertas. The is Project being conducted under the the “Enhanced Technical Assistance for Support Activities to Strengthen the European Integration Process Project” (ESEI) and 90 % of the Project budget is financed through EU funds while 10% shall be provided by Turkey. The purpose of the project which is to expand the administrative capacity of the Capital Markets Board by increasing investments realised in the private sector.
